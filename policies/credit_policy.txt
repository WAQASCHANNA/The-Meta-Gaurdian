CREDIT POLICY 2025

1. Loan Approval Limits
   - Maximum auto-approval amount: $50,000
   - Loans above $50,000 require manual review (High Risk).

2. Credit Score Requirements
   - Minimum credit score for approval: 600
   - Scores below 600 are automatically rejected.

3. Risk Assessment
   - High Risk: Amount > $50,000 OR Score < 600
   - Low Risk: Amount <= $50,000 AND Score >= 600
