HIRING POLICY 2025

1. Non-Discrimination
   - Decisions must not be based on location if the role is Remote.
   - Rejection based on "Unknown" location is flagged for review.

2. Experience Requirements
   - Minimum experience: 2 years for standard roles.
   - Candidates with < 2 years experience are rejected (Medium Risk).

3. Screening Process
   - All candidates must have a valid location specified.
