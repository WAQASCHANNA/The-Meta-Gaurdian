EXPENSE POLICY 2025

1. Approval Limits
   - Maximum auto-approval limit: $10,000
   - Expenses above $10,000 require VP approval (High Risk).

2. Category Restrictions
   - Software purchases above $5,000 require IT approval (Medium Risk).
   - Equipment purchases above $10,000 are flagged.

3. General
   - All expenses must be categorized.
